The Film

A Thesis Film in Final Post-Production

Scholastic Affair is a 9-minute dramatic thriller that unfolds in real-time on a stormy night, weaving together themes of gender, power, and the devastating consequences of secrets exposed.

Drawing from the shadow-soaked aesthetic of classic noir and the psychological intensity of Tennessee Williams, the film examines campus politics through a lens of moral ambiguity—where every character believes themselves justified, and no one escapes unscathed.

Shot in meticulous period detail, the parking lot becomes a stage for power dynamics: fluorescent lights hum with tension, rain-slicked asphalt reflects fractured loyalties, and a single car becomes the crucible for lives forever altered.
